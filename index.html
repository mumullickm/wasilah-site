Questions

A few quiet answers.

Everything we get asked most, in one place.

Is Wasilah made for Muslims in Bangladesh? +

Yes. Wasilah is built around Bangla support, BDT Zakat, local prayer needs, Qibla, Quran, Hadith, duas, and daily remembrance — for Bangladesh and Bengali-speaking Muslims everywhere.

Does Wasilah include Quran and Hadith in Bangla? +

Yes. Arabic, English, and Bangla are brought together, so Bengali-speaking Muslims can read, learn, and reflect in one place.

Can Wasilah calculate Zakat in BDT? +

Yes. The Zakat calculator is built around the Bangladeshi Taka — cash, gold, savings, business assets, and liabilities.

When is the app launching? +

Wasilah is preparing for an early Bangladesh launch on iOS and Android. Bookmark wasilah.site and you'll be the first to know.

Will Wasilah be free? +

Yes. Free to download and use — with no ads in the prayer or Quran experience.

Privacy

Quiet, by default.

Wasilah does not require an account. We do not sell or share personal data.

  • No account or sign-in required to use the site or app.
  • Location is requested only when you press "Use Location," and used only to compute prayer times in your browser.
  • Anonymous analytics help us improve the site. Your IP is anonymised.
  • Email us anytime to ask what data we hold, or to request deletion.
