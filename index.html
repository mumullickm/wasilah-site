    Why Wasilah

    Made for Bangladesh, not adapted for it.

    Quran, Hadith, duas and the 99 Names all read naturally in Bangla, alongside Arabic and English.

    Questions

    Wasilah Bangladesh FAQ

    Is Wasilah made for Muslims in Bangladesh?

    Yes. Wasilah is designed around Bangla support, BDT Zakat, local prayer needs, Qibla, Quran, Hadith, duas, and daily remembrance.

    Does Wasilah include Quran and Hadith in Bangla?

    Wasilah brings Arabic, English, and Bangla access together so Bengali-speaking Muslims can learn and reflect in one place.

    Can Wasilah calculate Zakat in BDT?

    Yes. The Zakat calculator is built around Bangladeshi Taka for cash, gold, savings, business assets, and liabilities.

    Is Wasilah available now?

    Yes. Wasilah is live now on the App Store and Google Play, free to install on any iPhone, iPad, or Android phone.

    Will Wasilah always be free?

    Yes. Wasilah is free to download and use, with no ads and no sign-up. It is sadaqah jariyah and will never be monetized.
